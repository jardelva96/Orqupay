# Audit Calendar (Template)

## Yearly plan (example)

1. January
- Update risk register and yearly audit plan.
- Review scope boundaries (CDE, production systems, shared services).

1. February
- Internal readiness check for SOC 2 controls.
- ISO 27001 internal audit cycle starts.

1. March
- Q1 DR drill and incident tabletop.
- Action plan review with executive sponsors.

1. April
- PCI pre-assessment with QSA (scope and gaps).
- Third-party risk review refresh.

1. May
- SOC 2 Type I evidence freeze and auditor kickoff.

1. June
- Q2 DR drill and segmentation test.

1. July
- Corrective actions sprint for QSA and SOC findings.

1. August
- SOC 2 Type II observation period checkpoint.

1. September
- Q3 DR drill and backup restore certification.

1. October
- ISO surveillance audit preparation.

1. November
- Q4 DR drill and full BCP rehearsal.

1. December
- Annual management review and next-year plan approval.
